Design Solution Option

Home › Design › Design Solution Option
Target turbidity < 1 NTU Low-maintenance Community operable Gravity / Solar ready

The water supply of Saibai Island is mainly dependent on lagoons and rainwater harvesting. In addition, the treatment water method of Saibai Island is mainly 5 μm and 1 μm filtration combined with chlorine disinfection. However, the turbidity of the treated water is often between 1–2 NTU, which does not meet TSIRC’s newly released requirement for water turbidity less than 1 NTU. Meanwhile, some residents disinfect by boiling, increasing both energy use and cost.

In summary, the main problem for communities is how to continue supplying safe drinking water in an affordable, maintainable, low-energy solution under climate and water-quality uncertainty, while reusing existing storage and distribution facilities as much as possible.

Solution 1 — Roof Rainwater (Non-potable)

Roof → gutter + leaf guard → storage tank → household use (toilets, garden).

Lowest energyEasy install

Solution 2 — Lagoon Filtration (Potable)

Mesh screen + swirl settler → 5 μm/1 μm + carbon → UF → disinfection.

Turbidity controlScalable

Compact modular unit providing pathogen barrier with simple visual checks.
