    Economic Analysis

    According to the census statistics in 2021, the median monthly salary is approximately 4,000 AUD, which is considerably higher than the total cost allocated per island resident (approximately 340 AUD) for this design.

    Among these costs, the long-term maintenance only requires staff to periodically replace consumable parts in the system, including activated carbon and filter cartridges once a month, which totally cost around 70 AUD. Therefore, even when labour costs (approximately 30 AUD per hour) are taken into account, the long-term maintenance cost of this system remains relatively low, with approximately only 100 AUD for each maintenance.

    Although the replacement of consumable materials and labour expenses should still be included in the overall cost assessment, these expenditures are minimal compared with those of conventional water treatment systems. As a result, the long-term maintenance cost can be considered low and economically manageable, which reflects the high cost-effectiveness of the design.
